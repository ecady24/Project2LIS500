Why I Took This Class

As someone entering the tech field, I wanted to better understand the social impact of systems often described as objective. This course pushed me to ask:

  • Who designs these systems?
  • What assumptions are embedded within them?
  • Who benefits or is harmed when those assumptions go unexamined?
