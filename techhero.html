Joy Buolamwini

Joy Buolamwini is a computer scientist, digital activist, and founder of the Algorithmic Justice League. Her work exposes bias in machine learning systems, especially in facial recognition software that performs worse on women and people of color.

While studying at the MIT Media Lab, Buolamwini found that many commercial AI systems struggled to identify her face until she wore a lighter mask. This experience inspired her research into algorithmic bias and fairness.

Through the Algorithmic Justice League, she advocates for more equitable and accountable AI systems. Her work combines research, storytelling, and policy engagement to address the harms caused by biased technology.
